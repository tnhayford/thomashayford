Health and Safety Officer
Location ; Dzorwulu

Requirement;
*Degree in related field
*Minimum qualification in HSE
*Practical understanding of QHSE management systems
(ISO 9001;2015, ISO45001; 2018& ISO 14001;2015)
*A Valid Driving License
*2-3 years of practical QHSE experience


Duties;
*To develop, implement and monitor Top management Quality, Occupational Health and Safety Policy.
*To assist the Top management in complying with current health and safety legislation and/or regulations with the objective of ensuring that all reasonable and proper measures are taken to protect the safety and health of staff and visitors;
*To establish budget proposals for the operation of the QHSE department and specific training programs;
*To increase health and safety, environment and quality management awareness at all levels within the organization;
*To investigate and report on all serious/critical personal injury accidents occurring to staff and/or visitors to the appropriate senior official, and to assist in the investigation of all
*Accidents/incidents that result in substantial damage to Board vehicles and property;
* To respond to employees’ safety concerns;
*To respond to fires and other emergencies on or about the company property; office/warehouse etc.
*To receive reports from and respond to orders issued by Department of Labor inspectors;
*To arrange for Occupational Health and Safety testing and/or evaluations of the workplace and
warehouse by external agencies/consultants as may be necessary;
*To act as liaison with all related governmental bodies and regulating agencies

Salary; Open for Negotiation
Apply to jobs@thehrbicltd.com
Deadline; 10th August, 2023

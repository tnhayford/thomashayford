Location: Adoteiman, Accra

A reputable poultry farm in Adoteiman is looking for Salespersons to support the marketing and distribution of its poultry products. This role is ideal for individuals who are results-driven, customer-focused, and passionate about agricultural sales.

Promote and sell poultry products to both existing and new customers.

Build and maintain strong relationships with clients, retailers, and local markets.

Keep accurate records of sales, payments, and customer interactions.

Assist with coordinating deliveries and managing product stock levels.

Gather customer feedback and market trends to help improve products and services.

Prior experience in sales, marketing, or customer service is an advantage.

Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Self-motivated, with the ability to meet sales targets and work independently.

SSNIT registration for social security benefits.

Attractive commissions based on performance.

NHIS registration for healthcare access.

Training and support to help you succeed in your role.

Opportunity for career growth within a reputable agricultural business.

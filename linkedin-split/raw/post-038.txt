WE HAVE VACANCY FOR TRANSPORT OFFICER - LOGISTICS SECTOR
Industry: Logistics & Transportation

Job Overview:
 We are seeking an experienced and proactive Transport Officer to oversee the efficient coordination of heavy-duty trucks, trailers, and general fleet operations. This role demands hands-on expertise in logistics management, route planning, driver supervision, and adherence to safety and compliance regulations.

Key Responsibilities:
Supervise daily fleet operations including truck assignments, maintenance schedules, and delivery timelines.
Monitor vehicle performance, fuel usage, and repair costs to ensure efficiency.
Liaise with clients to ensure timely delivery of goods and services.
Maintain transport logs, regulatory documentation, and internal performance reports.
Ensure all vehicles meet roadworthiness, insurance, and safety requirements.
Coordinate with HR and Admin for recruitment, training, and welfare of drivers.

Qualifications & Experience:
Bachelor’s degree or HND in Logistics, Transport Management, or related field.
Minimum 3 years of transport/fleet management experience (preferably in FMCG, manufacturing, or logistics companies).
Strong understanding of truck operations, axle loads, trailer specs, and related logistics.
Proficiency in fleet tracking tools and Microsoft Office.
Excellent organizational and leadership skills.

To Apply: Send your CV to recruitment@reliance.com.gh with the subject line “Transport Officer – Logistics”
 Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

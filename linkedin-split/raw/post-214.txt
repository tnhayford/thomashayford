The Africa Transitional Justice Legacy Fund (ATJLF) is an international organization which supports local initiatives, through grant making, capacity building, convening and communities of practice, to reduce barriers to fair and accessible justice in transitional societies. The African Union (AU), in collaboration with the European Union (EU) Delegation to the African Union in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, is undertaking a 3-year project called the "Initiative for Transitional Justice in Africa." The primary objective of the project is to support the implementation of the African Union Transitional Justice Policy and its roadmap. The project is being implemented in partnership with a consortium comprised of the International Center for Transitional Justice (ICTJ), the African Transitional Justice Legacy Fund (ATJLF), and the Center for the Study of Violence and Reconciliation (CSVR).

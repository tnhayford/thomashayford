Mantrac Ghana Limited is hashtag#hiring for the position of IT Manager


The IT Manager at Mantrac Ghana Limited serves as the senior-most IT professional
at the Business Unit (BU) level, responsible for ensuring reliable and secure
technology operations across the country. This role acts as the key liaison with the
Mantrac Group IT Centre of Excellence (CoE), supporting the execution of the Group

IT standards. The incumbent leads a team of four professionals, overseeing IT
support, asset management, and local IT services and system performance.

managerial capacity.

👉Desirable:

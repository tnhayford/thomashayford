JOB VACANCY: ACCOUNT OFFICER – KUMASI

A reputable company in Kumasi is seeking to hire a dedicated and experienced Account Officer to join our team. The ideal candidate must have a strong background in accounting, excellent attention to detail, and the ability to work independently.

Location: Kumasi, Ghana

Position: Account Officer

Key Responsibilities:
 • Maintain and update financial records and accounting systems
 • Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
 • Monitor cash flow and assist in budget planning
 • Reconcile bank statements and resolve accounting discrepancies
 • Ensure compliance with all financial regulations and company policies
 • Assist with audits, tax filings, and inventory records
 • Liaise with external auditors, suppliers, and bank

Qualifications & Skills:
 • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 • Minimum of 3 years of practical accounting experience
 • Proficiency in accounting software (e.g. Tally, QuickBooks, Sage)
 • Strong knowledge of Ghanaian tax laws and statutory requirements
 • Excellent analytical, organizational, and communication skills
 • High level of integrity and professionalism

How to Apply:

Interested applicants should send their CV and cover letter to hr@salompharmaceuticals.com with the subject line “Account Officer - Kumasi” no later than 31st July, 2024

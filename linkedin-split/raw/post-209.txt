Job Opportunity: BE Developer - Experienced Mid/Senior-level

We seek an experienced Backend (BE) Developer with strong expertise in Node.js to join our dynamic and innovative team. As a BE Developer, you will play a crucial role in developing and integrating ERP systems with social media platforms such as Facebook and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems. If you possess exceptional technical skills, have a passion for solving complex challenges, and can communicate effectively at a very high level, this opportunity is for you!

Key Responsibilities:

- Develop and maintain robust and scalable backend applications using Node.js.
- Integrate ERP systems with popular social media platforms like Facebook and CRM systems.
- Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to understand client requirements and deliver efficient solutions.
- Ensure code quality, performance, and security through code reviews and best practices.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues to optimise system performance.

Requirements:

- Proven experience as a Backend Developer with expertise in Node.js (4+ years experience).
- Strong communication skills with the ability to interact effectively with clients and stakeholders.
- Experience in integrating ERP systems with Facebook and CRM platforms is a significant advantage.
- Fluent in English with a B2/C1 level or higher.
- A proactive problem-solver with a passion for continuous learning and innovation.

Why Work With Us:

Join a talented and diverse team of professionals in a collaborative and supportive work environment. Contribute to cutting-edge projects that impact various industries and clients worldwide. Ongoing opportunities for professional growth and skill development.
Competitive compensation package and benefits.

Location: Accra, GNAT Heights

How to Apply:

If you are ready to take on exciting challenges and thrive in a fast-paced, technology-driven environment, we want to hear from you! Send your updated CV to recruitment@remoteli.co.uk. Please include "BE Developer - Node.js" in the subject line.

We are looking forward to having you as a part of our talented team!

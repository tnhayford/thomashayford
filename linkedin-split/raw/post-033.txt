WE HAVE VACANCY FOR A WAREHOUSE OFFICER - AUTOMOTIVE PARTS MANUFACTURING 
(Accounting Background Preferred)
Location: Accra
Industry: Automotive Parts / Manufacturing

Job Overview:
We are looking for a detail-oriented and disciplined Warehouse Officer with a solid understanding of inventory control and a strong accounting foundation. The ideal candidate will maintain accurate records of goods and materials while aligning warehouse operations with financial reporting standards.

Key Responsibilities:
Receive, inspect, and store raw materials and finished goods.
Maintain up-to-date inventory databases using Excel or ERP systems.
Prepare monthly stock reconciliation and inventory valuation reports.
Monitor stock movements and report discrepancies promptly.
Implement safety procedures and ensure warehouse compliance with standards.
Support finance in audits and costing-related tasks.

Qualifications & Experience:
HND or Bachelor’s in Accounting, Logistics, or Supply Chain.
At least 2 years’ experience in a similar role within a warehouse or manufacturing setting.
Strong Excel and data analysis skills.
Knowledge of stock-taking procedures and documentation.
Familiarity with cost accounting is an advantage.

To Apply:
Send your CV to recruitment@reliance.com.gh with the subject line “Warehouse Officer – Manufacturing”
 
Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

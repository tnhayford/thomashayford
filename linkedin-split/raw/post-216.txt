Job Title - Finance Officer

Join Our Team - Finance Officer 
Are you ready to make a significant impact in the financial management of a dynamic organization? 
We are a firm that establishes rainforest through restoring degraded land using our unique restoration approaches, we are seeking a young talented and experienced Finance Officer to join our 
team in Bibiani, Western North Region of Ghana. 
Position: Finance Officer 
Location: Bibiani, Western North Region of Ghana 
Employment Type: Full-time 
Functions of Appointment: 
• Finalize departmental monthly reports for entry in Xero 
• Prepare monthly bank reconciliations for entry in Xero 
• Post all transactions into accounting system 
• Responsible for preparation of employees’ monthly salaries 
• Responsible for payment of all statutory deductions (Pensions & PAYE) 
• Receive, verify and process invoices for payment and correct posting 
• Review and analyze all supporting documentation regarding payments 
• Report any misconduct or violations of internal control standards directly to GFC, MD and/or 
CoCEO 
• Assist with end of year preparation and audit preparation 
• Check & process staff out of pocket expenses 
• Assist in preparing monthly reporting package 
• Update asset register and verify physical assets 
• Maintain the company accounts 
• Ensure that company finance internal control standards are followed (e.g. approval limits, 
documentation of company transactions) 
• Process and supervise all necessary bank & cash payments 
• Process and supervise monthly payroll accounting 
• Point of contact for external stakeholders (e.g. Ghana Revenue Authority, SSNIT, external 
auditors) 
• Responsible for maintaining VAT, NHIL and Getfund Reports. 
• Responsible for submitting monthly VAT, NHIL and Getfund returns to Ghana Revenue
Authority. 
• Assist Group Financial Controller in cash flow forecast planning 
• Assist Group Financial Controller in preparing of monthly reporting package 
Job Holder Specification 
Formal Education: 
• At least a Bachelor’s in Accounting/finance or related field 
Experience: At least three years in a similar role 
Personal qualities and competencies: 
• Ability to interpret the accounting standards 
• Good in Excel, Word, Publisher and PowerPoint presentation 
• Highest standards of ethics and confidentiality at work 
• Work independently in the absence of supervision 
• Communicate clearly and concisely, both orally and in writing 
• Ability to implement internal control policies effectively 
• Establish and maintain effective working relations with all stakeholders 
• Respond to requests and inquiries from line management and employees effectively and 
Timeously 
Duty Station: Bibiani, Western North Region 
How to Apply: 
 Send Resume, cover letter, and any relevant certifications TO resourcestriangle@gmail.com. 
Application Deadline: Tuesday, 15th August, 2023

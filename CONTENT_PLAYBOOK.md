## Publishing Cadence
Minimum weekly cadence:
- 1 business post
- 1 personal experience or hard moment post
- 1 travel or fun post

Target: 3 posts per week, each with unique angle and details.

## Content Pillars
- `business`: systems, product, leadership, operations
- `experience`: milestones, lessons, turning points
- `travel`: locations, perspective shifts, observations
- `fun`: human side, hobbies, social routines
- `hard-moments`: failures, pressure, recovery plans

## SEO Metadata Rules (Strict)
For every post:
- Unique `<title>` with target phrase near the front
- Unique `<meta name="description">` (130-160 chars preferred)
- Canonical URL set to final post URL
- `BlogPosting` JSON-LD with accurate date and section
- At least 2 internal links to related pages
- Use one consistent author name: `Thomas Hayford`
- Add to `sitemap.xml`

## URL and Slug Rules
- Lowercase only
- Use hyphens, no spaces
- Include year when helpful for uniqueness
- Example: `/journal/business/scaling-operations-with-a-small-team-2026/`

## Media Rules
- Use original photos where possible
- File naming format: `thomas-hayford-[topic]-[yyyy-mm].jpg`
- Add descriptive alt text

## Distribution Workflow Per Post
1. Publish post page.
2. Add link card to `/journal/index.html` and category page.
3. Add URL to `/sitemap.xml`.
4. Share on LinkedIn with canonical link.
5. Request indexing in Google Search Console.

## Disambiguation Rules

- Keep an up-to-date `/who-is-thomas-hayford/` page
- Keep CV page and PDF accessible
- Keep consistent references to Iderwell and role
- Keep the same profile photo and bio text across external profiles
